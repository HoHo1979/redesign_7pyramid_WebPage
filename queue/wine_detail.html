Château Margaux 2010

A vintage of monumental structure and ethereal elegance. The 2010 represents the pinnacle of the Margaux profile, balancing sheer power with an unmatched aromatic complexity.

Vintage Notes

The Architectural Season

The 2010 growing season was characterized by an exceptional drought, which resulted in small, concentrated berries with remarkably high levels of tannin and acidity. This structural core is what defines the longevity of this specific archive.

Harvest took place under crystal clear skies, allowing the Cabernet Sauvignon to reach a level of phenolic maturity rarely seen in the Médoc. The result is a wine of profound density that remarkably retains the floral delicacy synonymous with the estate.
